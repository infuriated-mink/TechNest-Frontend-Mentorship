Joey Tribbiani

About Joey

Joey Tribbiani, played by Matt LeBlanc, is an aspiring actor best known for his role as Dr. Drake Ramoray on *Days of Our Lives*. He is Monica and Chandler's roommate and the group’s lovable foodie.

Fun Facts

  • His catchphrase is “How you doin’?”
  • Loves pizza and sandwiches more than anything.
  • Has a soft heart despite his goofy personality.
